Arantxa Astudillo
Tatara Abuela
Acrylic on Canvas
30X30''
June 2016

Tatara Abuela

Tatara abuelameans great grandmother in Spanish. I never knew my late great grandmother. One day, I just wanted to paint, so I let my brush take the lead. I painted this old lady, who looks just like my late great grandmother, a smoker.

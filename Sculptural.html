Rock and Roll

Arantxa Astudillo
Laser cut wood
2019

I designed 11 pieces that are modularly "fit" to one another. The designs consists of four characters: a "hammerhead shark", a "seahorse", an "angelfish", and a "blobfish". I also designed to ways which the pieces can move, one my rocking, and another by rolling.



Lavalounertesorisous

Arantxa Astudillo
3d print and homegrown crystals
1"X4"
2019

This miniature sculpture is a mythological creature of my own invention. It has the body of a snake, the tail of a piranha,and no head. It lives deep inside the Earth and when it dies, it crystalizes.

Light of Life

Arantxa Astudillo
Welded steel rods
24X24''
2017

I was inspired by the beauty of the changes the female body experiences during and after a pregnancy. Each individual wire is the silhouette of the breasts and stomach of a woman; the wires change shape, like the body of the woman.

Elephant Toy

Arantxa Astudillo
Wood
12X24''
2017

This sculpture can be taken apart, piece by piece. It can be used as a toy; elephants can be used as a tool and they too can be torn apart, mentally and physically. I have seen and touched elephants in Thailand and I could see the pain that they have endured in their past, yet, there they were, trusting humans once again.

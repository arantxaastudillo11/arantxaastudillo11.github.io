ARANTXA ASTUDILLO

Artist Statement

In my early 20’s I promised myself to travel to at least one new country each year. So far I have kept my promise and in return I have gained an immense amount of cultural and environmental knowledge, respect and creative inspiration. Throughout my travels I have noted extreme contrasts between my own home in California and the environments and cultures of the countries I have visited. I suddenly became aware of cultural and environmental diversity in a way I could not have, had I not witnessed it first hand. As an animal and nature lover, I often include natural materials, animal rights activism, and environmental awareness in my pieces. Through my art, I strive to reflect the phenomena, horrors and beauty of diversity in a visually pleasing and at times humorous ways.

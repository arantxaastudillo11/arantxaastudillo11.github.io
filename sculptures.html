Arantxa Astudillo
Light of Life
Welded steel wire rods
24X24''
October 2017

Light of Life

I was inspired by the beauty of the changes the female body experiences during and after a pregnancy. Each individual wire is the silhouette of the breasts and stomach of a woman; the wires change shape, like the body of the woman.

Arantxa Astudillo
Elephant Toy
Wood panels and
wooden rods
24X24''
October 2017

Elephant Toy

This sculpture can be taken apart, piece by piece. It can be used as a toy; elephants can be used as a tool and they too can be torn apart, mentally and physically. I have seen and touched elephants in Thailand and I could see the pain that they have endured in their past, yet, there they were, trusting humans once again.

Bullet Tusks

Arantxa Astudillo
PINtacle of Pain
Pins and hat
12X5''
February 2018

PINtacle of Pain

A hat, my protection from the sun. Hurting me, yet I need it to protect me. Many things is life are like this; we allow them to hurt us, because it benefits us in a completely different way. Maybe the hat is a tool you use to look more appealing, yet you know it is only hiding your insecurities, hurting you.

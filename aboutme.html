About the Artist

Arantxa Astudillo, an artist based in the heart of the Silicon Valley, immigrated from Chile when she was only 8 years old. She is currently working on her undergraduate degree in Digital Media Art at San Jose Sate University.

As an immigrant and world traveler, Arantxa incorporates variety of cultures into her digital work.


Artist Statement

In my young adult life, I have exposed myself to places and situations that were rather extraordinary for me. Throughout my travels around the world, I have noted extreme contrasts between my own home in California and the environments and culture of countries I have visited; from the street conditions, homes, cultures, wildlife, ecosystems, poverty- I suddenly became aware of cultural and environmental diversity in a way I could not have, had I not witnessed it first hand. This contrast became my passion and inspiration. My art strives to reflect the phenomena of diversity that so many people are unaware of. I’d like to shine a light on the beauty and the horrors that often go unseen to the American eye. I’d like to expose the differences that many have been obscured from. Digital art is something I became exposed to when I began working at Best Buy. I helped install a virtual reality game, which I then played. I was instantly amazed!
